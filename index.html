About Laura

Precision, Integrity, and Professionalism

Laura Piccolo is a California commissioned notary public and certified loan signing agent based in Pleasant Hill, CA. A lifelong Bay Area resident who grew up in Walnut Creek, Laura proudly serves clients throughout the East Bay and Tri-Valley communities.

She specializes in loan signings and estate planning documents, working with escrow officers, attorneys, real estate professionals, and the general public to ensure documents are executed accurately and efficiently.
